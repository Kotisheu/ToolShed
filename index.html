Anna Akana is an actress and filmmaker strengthening her craft by creating short films in 2014 before tackling a feature. Though still in the process of finding her voice and honing in on the balance between acting and directing, she's already mastered the art of organization and crew-pleasing crafty. Most of what consistently appeals to her are dark scifi, fantastical comedy, and anything Stephen King.
