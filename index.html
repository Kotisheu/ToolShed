What are we?

The Tool Shed is a workshop that provides space and equipment for woodworking and apparel making for intermediate to advanced level crafters. As the popularity of DIY (do-it-yourself) projects continues to increase, many people are seeking ways to make their own customized creations. By paying a monthly membership fee, our members are able to utilize quality equipment that may be too expensive or unwieldy for individuals to own in their homes. Members are also able to purchase materials to create their projects from our workshop and sell their creations at our in-house boutique if they choose to do so.
